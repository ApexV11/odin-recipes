Lasagna Recepie

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce. It's a hearty and comforting meal that's perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

How to make Lasagna step by step:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the sausage and ground beef over medium heat until browned.
  3. Add the onion and garlic, cooking until softened.
  4. Stir in the tomato products, sugar, and spices. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  6. In a bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, egg, and some of the mozzarella cheese.
  7. Layer the ingredients in a baking dish: meat sauce, noodles, cheese mixture, and repeat.
  8. Top with remaining mozzarella cheese.
  9. Bake for 45 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.
  10. Let it cool for 15 minutes before serving.
